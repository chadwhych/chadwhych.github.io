About me

Chad Whych is a rising fourth year student at The University of Virginia. A Media Studies major with minors in both American Government & Social Entrepreneurship. He is veteran writer for The Cavalier Daily (UVA’s premier student ran newspaper) where he has covered numerous sports contents, mainly focusing on wrestling and football. Along with this, Chad has gained hands on experience as an intern for the ACC Network, through work in various roles associated with live sporting event production including; assistant director, camera operator, sideline reporter, research prep, graphics, replay and more.

In addition to his class, internship, and newspaper work, Chad has focused his career path in the field of marketing, advertisement, social media, and communications. He has diversified himself in these fields through both his traditional curriculum and through taking courses from McIntire School of Commerce’s business institute & Radify Labs. Both of which provided him with skills including: Marketing, Accounting, Finance & Excel, business strategy, and graphic design.Furthermore, Chad has intermediate skills in numerous Adobe Programs including Premier & Photoshop.

Now Chad is seeking to take his skills into the business world. He is an extremely diverse individual with useful skills for both the public and private sector. His main goal is to acquire a marketing/sales, consulting, or media production position in the sports business world. However, he is well equipped to work in any other business setting or a public sector position. He is an extremely hard-working young man who works great in teams. He will use any of his aforementioned skills to the of his best ability in order to assist any given company with their day to day objectives and goals.
